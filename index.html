    Register to Vote

    Everything you need to know about getting on the electoral roll.

    Required Documents

    • Proof of Age: Birth certificate, Pan Card, Aadhaar Card, or Class 10 Marksheet.
    • Proof of Residence: Passport, Ration Card, Aadhaar Card, or utility bills (Water/Electricity).
    • Passport Size Photograph

    How to Apply Online (Form 6)

    1. Visit the official Voter Service Portal (voters.eci.gov.in).
    2. Sign up or log in using your mobile number.
    3. Select "Fill Form 6" for new voter registration.
    4. Upload the required documents and submit. You will receive a reference number to track your application.

    Fake News Check

    How to verify election-related claims and avoid misinformation.

    Common Types of Election Misinformation

    • Fake Poll Timings: Messages claiming polling dates or times have changed. Always verify with ECI.
    • Bogus Voter Deletions: Rumors that entire communities have been deleted from rolls. Check your status online.
    • Altered Videos/Deepfakes: Manipulated videos of politicians saying things they never did. Check trusted news sources.

    How to Verify Information

    1. Check the Source: Is the information from an official ECI handle or website (eci.gov.in)?
    2. Look for Fact-Checkers: Use trusted fact-checking platforms to verify viral claims.
    3. Don't Forward Blindly: If a message evokes strong emotions or asks you to forward it urgently, it is likely fake. Stop the chain.
